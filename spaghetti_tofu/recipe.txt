Simple Tofu Spaghetti Ragu

    Prep time: 10 mins
    Cook time: 20 mins
    Servings: 4

Instructions

    Prep Tofu: Press the tofu for 10 minutes to remove excess water, then crumble into small pieces with a fork.
    Cook Pasta: Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package directions.
    Sauté Tofu: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Fry the tofu crumbs for 5–7 minutes until slightly golden.
    Make Sauce: Add onion, garlic, and sundried tomatoes to the tofu. Sauté until tender (3-5 mins). Stir in tomato puree and simmer for 1-2 minutes.
    Simmer: Pour in the tomato sauce and add nutritional yeast, salt, and herbs. Let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
    Combine: Drain the pasta (reserve some pasta water) and stir it directly into the sauce. Add reserved water to loosen the sauce if needed.
    Serve: Garnish with fresh basil and vegan parmesan.
